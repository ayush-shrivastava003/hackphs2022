about

What is hackPHS?

hackPHS hosts over 300 high school students from across New Jersey to collaborate, innovate, and create in a 24-hour, student organized hackathon based at Princeton High School. Whether you're a complete beginner or an experienced hacker, hackPHS will have something for you!

When and Where is hackPHS?

CHANGE THIS!!!!! hackPHS will be a hybrid event this year, running from November 6th, 9 a.m. to November 7th, 1 p.m. Princeton High School students will be welcome to attend the in-person event in the cafeteria of Princeton High School, located at 151 Moore St, Princeton, NJ (but are not required to), and the rest of the participants will be attending remotely via a discord server. The in-person portion of the event will occur from 9 a.m. to 12 a.m. on the 6th and 10 a.m. to 1 p.m. on the 7th. Students coming in person will be required to return home at 12 am and will be welcomed back at 10 am the next day. In person safety precauntions will include mask wearing, table dividers, eating outdoors, social distancing, and hand sanitizer stations.

Who pays for all of this?

hackPHS is completely free to everyone, thanks to our amazing sponsors!

What is a hackathon?

Hackathons are where creative and enthusiastic people come together to build awesome projects in a single all-nighter. With awesome workshops and incredible prizes, hackers will learn about new tech, build innovative solutions to real-world problems, and share their creation with the community, all in one event. If you are looking for a competition, a place to meet fellow hackers, or even just a place to learn how to code, hackathons are for you. If you want to learn more, see the MLH Hackathon FAQ.

faq

How will we get our swag?

As long as you attend at least one workshop or submit your project to devpost, you will receive swag. This includes t-shirts, stickers, etc. In person participants will receive their swag at the event, and online participants will have their swag mailed no matter where in the world they live!

Who can attend this event?

This event is for high school students only. If you are no longer a high schooler and would like to help out, consider applying to be a mentor!

How do I apply?

Apply by going to our clipboard. Swag will be distributed on a first-come first-serve basis, so apply ASAP!
